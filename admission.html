Admissions

Register Your Interest With Us in a Few Simple Steps:

  1. Application

    Complete an Application Form with your child's school reports attached.

  2. Assessment / Taster Day

    Kindergarten students are welcome to join a taster day and are assessed by the Head of the Kindergarten's section.

  3. Interview

    Year 3 to Year 12 / IFY students are invited to attend an interview with the Head of Year before meeting with the Headteacher.

  4. Offer Letter

    An offer letter is sent to parents and legal guardians.

  5. Registration & Caution Money

    Parents/guardians pay a registration fee of KSh xxxxx per child.

  6. Admission

    Your child is admitted to Roshani International School.
